# Brisbane North

Set in the bustling capital city of Queensland, Australia, circa 2023, North Brisbane represents the busy North Coast Main Line, as well as suburban branch lines to the leafy Ferny Grove; the twin race courses at Doomben; Brisbane Airport, Australia's third busiest; quiet bayside Shorncliffe; and the ever-growing Redcliffe Peninsula. Negotiate carefully-timed sequencing at numerous flat junctions to try and keep this network running on time!

## Simulation

Multiple timetables are included representing the railway on a weekday in different three hour time periods, with services from all branches.
